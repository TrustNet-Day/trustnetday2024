Trust and Privacy in Networks (TRUSTNET DAY) 2024 – May 23-24, Limoges

Event description

Welcome to the second edition of Trustnet Day, an event that aims to gather academics and members of the industry, allowing them to discuss topics related to the notion of trust!

For this second edition, we are organizing a two-day event in Limoges, with a Capture the Flag event scheduled in the afternoon between the two conference days. The first day of the event will mostly feature academic presentations on four main topics:

  • Evolving trust assumptions: quantum-resistant cryptography
  • Trust through Attestation
  • Trust and user Privacy
  • Blockchain, IoT, and Trust

Please check our Call for Papers and submit your extended abstract now! And of course, if you enjoy challenges in cybersecurity and cryptography, please consider register a team in our CTF event (first come first registered).

We particularly encourage young researchers (Ph.D. students, post-doctoral researchers, etc.) to take part and share results. Our workshop will have no proceedings, so you can also describe work in progress or as of yet unpublished.

Registration (deadline May 18th, 2024)
